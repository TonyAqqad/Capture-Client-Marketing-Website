## Pricing Strategy Recommendations

### Current Pricing Problem
- **Starter:** $999/month (50 calls) = $20/call
- **My AI Front Desk:** $99/month (200 mins) = $0.33/call
- **We're 60x more expensive per call**

### Recommended Pricing Structure

#### Option 1: Add Entry Tier (Recommended)
```
Solo Professional: $399/month (30 calls)
Starter: $999/month (100 calls) - increase calls
Growth: $1,997/month (300 calls) - increase calls
Enterprise: $3,997/month (unlimited qualified calls)
```
**Rationale:** Captures low-end market without devaluing premium tiers

#### Option 2: Unbundle Services
```
Voice AI Only: $399/month (compete with My AI Front Desk)
Voice AI + Basic Ads: $999/month (current Starter)
Full-Service (AI + Ads + CRM): $1,997/month (premium)
```
**Rationale:** Gives customers choice, clarifies what they're paying for

#### Option 3: Usage-Based Pricing
```
Platform Fee: $199/month
+ $5 per qualified call (not just answered calls)
Estimated cost: $399-1999/month depending on volume
```
**Rationale:** Aligns pricing with value delivered (qualified leads, not just answers)

### Recommended: Hybrid Approach
- Keep current premium tiers for "Full-Service" (Voice AI + Ads + CRM)
- Add "Voice AI Only" tier at $399/month to compete directly
- Position as "DIY vs Done-For-You"
