# Kentucky Cities - Deep Local Research for Content Localization

**Population:** 630,000+ (metro area)
**Economic Focus:** Healthcare hub (Humana HQ, Norton Healthcare), logistics (UPS Worldport), bourbon industry, manufacturing

**Key Business Districts:**
- **Medical Center Row** - Hospital district with Norton Healthcare, UofL Health
- **Whiskey Row** - Historic downtown bourbon distillery district
- **The Highlands** - Restaurant and retail corridor along Bardstown Rd
- **St. Matthews** - Major commercial district, professional offices
- **Oxmoor Center area** - Corporate offices, healthcare
- **East End** - Professional services, corporate offices (Brownsboro Rd)

**Business Centers/Office Parks:**
- Watterson Business District
- Oxmoor Corporate Center
- Bluegrass Commerce Park
- Louisville East Technology Park
- Riverport Business Park

**Chamber:** Greater Louisville Inc. (GLI)

**Local Context:** Derby City, Churchill Downs, Fourth Street Live, Waterfront Park

## LOCALIZATION STRATEGY

For each city, inject:
1. **Specific business district names** in intro paragraphs
2. **Office park/business center names** for local credibility
3. **Chamber of Commerce mentions** for trust signals
4. **Unique local context** (Derby City, Horse Capital, Corvette plant, etc.)
5. **Geographic landmarks** that locals recognize
6. **Industry-specific callouts** (bourbon in Louisville, horses in Lexington, Corvette in Bowling Green)

**Pattern:**
"From the [business district] to the [landmark area], [city] businesses are [action]. Whether you're serving [specific neighborhood] or managing [type of business] in [area], our AI voice agents..."

**Examples:**
- Louisville: "From Medical Center Row to the offices along Brownsboro Road, Louisville's healthcare and professional services are embracing AI..."
- Lexington: "Along the Nicholasville Road corridor and in the offices near CentrePointe, Lexington businesses are discovering..."
- Bowling Green: "From the Scottsville Road corridor to businesses near the Corvette plant, Bowling Green companies..."
- Owensboro: "Whether you're serving the Frederica Street commercial district or the downtown riverfront, Owensboro businesses..."
- Covington: "From Mainstrasse Village to the RiverCenter towers overlooking Cincinnati, Northern Kentucky businesses..."
