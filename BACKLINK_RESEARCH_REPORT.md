## COMPETITOR BACKLINK REVERSE ENGINEERING

### How to Steal Competitor Backlinks

**Step 1:** Identify top 5 competitors ranking for your target keywords
- Use Google search: "voice ai agency"
- Use SEMrush/Ahrefs to find competitors

**Step 2:** Export their backlink profiles
- Ahrefs: Enter competitor domain → Backlinks → Export
- Filter for DA 40+, dofollow only

**Step 3:** Categorize their backlinks
- Directories (replicate easily)
- Guest posts (pitch same sites)
- Resource pages (request addition)
- News mentions (similar PR angles)
- Partnerships (explore same opportunities)

**Step 4:** Prioritize replication
- Focus on links you can realistically obtain
- Skip one-off editorial mentions (hard to replicate)
- Prioritize directories, guest post sites, resource pages

**Step 5:** Execute outreach
- Use competitor's success as social proof: "You featured [Competitor], I think our agency would be a great fit too because..."
