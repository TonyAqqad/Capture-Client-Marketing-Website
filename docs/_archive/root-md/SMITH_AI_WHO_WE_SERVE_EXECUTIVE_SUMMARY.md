## Complete Industry List

### 1. Professional Services

#### Legal & Law Firms
- **Sub-types:** Solo practitioners, small law firms, personal injury, family law, criminal defense, estate law
- **Key Pain Points:** 67% of clients judge on initial responsiveness, 25% of day lost to interruptions
- **Core Features:** Legal intake with conflict checking, appointment scheduling, payment collection
- **ROI:** $77K/year in-house receptionist vs. $95-292/month

#### Real Estate Agents
- **Sub-types:** Residential, commercial, buyer's agents, seller's agents, brokers
- **Key Pain Points:** Calls during showings/negotiations, 50% of lead budgets wasted
- **Core Features:** Lead qualification (budget/timeline), 24/7 showing booking, CRM automation
- **ROI:** $40K-70K saved annually vs. in-house reception

#### Marketing & Design Agencies
- **Sub-types:** Marketing agencies, design firms, graphic design, social media agencies, PR firms, photography
- **Key Pain Points:** Focus on client campaigns disrupted, ROI tracking difficult
- **Core Features:** Lead capture, CRM integration proves marketing ROI
- **Impact:** 22% improvement in marketing ROI with AI integration

#### Staffing & Recruiting
- **Sub-types:** Staffing agencies, recruiting firms, executive search, temp agencies, workforce development
- **Key Pain Points:** After-hours candidate contact, peak hiring season overload
- **Core Features:** 24/7 screening, interview scheduling, CRM integration

#### Insurance Agencies
- **Sub-types:** Independent agents, brokers, life/health/property & casualty, commercial insurance
- **Key Pain Points:** Overwhelming lead volume, follow-up complexity
- **Core Features:** Lead vetting, appointment scheduling, 24/7 availability

#### Accounting & Bookkeeping
- **Sub-types:** Accountants, bookkeepers, CPAs, tax preparation, payroll services
- **Key Pain Points:** Seasonal spikes (tax season), administrative burden
- **Core Features:** Client intake, appointment scheduling, payment collection

#### Financial Advisors & Services
- **Sub-types:** Financial advisors, lenders, mortgage brokers, investment advisors, wealth management
- **Key Pain Points:** Interrupted client meetings, complex intake requirements
- **Core Features:** Structured intake (financial goals, risk tolerance), lead qualification

### 2. Home Services (Largest Category)

#### HVAC, Plumbing, Electrical, General Contractors
- **Sub-types:** HVAC contractors, plumbers, electricians, general contractors, carpentry, painters, locksmiths, pool services
- **Key Pain Points:** 50% of lead budgets wasted, technicians can't answer while working, emergencies need immediate response
- **Core Features:** 24/7 emergency routing, appointment automation, payment collection, Housecall Pro/ServiceTitan integration
- **ROI:** 60-80% cost reduction vs. administrative staff, starting $95/month

#### Pest Control
- **Sub-types:** Residential/commercial pest control, termite, wildlife removal, bed bugs
- **Key Pain Points:** Emergency situations, seasonal spikes, inspection scheduling
- **Core Features:** Pest severity assessment, 24/7 emergency handling, treatment tracking

#### Cleaning Services
- **Sub-types:** House cleaning, commercial, carpet, window, move-out, deep cleaning
- **Key Pain Points:** Crews can't answer during jobs, appointment conflicts
- **Core Features:** AI-first booking, payment collection, CRM integration

#### Landscaping & Garden Services
- **Sub-types:** Lawn care, landscape design, tree services, irrigation, hardscaping
- **Key Pain Points:** Seasonal variations, emergency irrigation issues
- **Core Features:** Seasonal call intelligence, neighborhood appointment routing

#### Roofing Services
- **Sub-types:** Residential roofing, commercial roofing, storm damage, inspections
- **Key Pain Points:** Estimate appointment scheduling, emergency storm calls
- **Core Features:** Storm damage intake, inspection scheduling, lead capture

#### Construction & Building Design
- **Sub-types:** Architects, general construction, building design, solar energy, custom homes
- **Key Pain Points:** Project managers on-site, bid requests after hours
- **Core Features:** Estimate scheduling, project inquiry management, lead screening

### 3. Healthcare & Wellness

#### Medical & Wellness Practices
- **Sub-types:** General medical, dental, orthodontists, chiropractors, veterinary, wellness centers, laser clinics, spas, massage, sleep centers, rehab
- **Key Pain Points:** No-shows impact revenue, 38% of dental calls missed, $1.7B annual ER costs for dental pain
- **Core Features:** Appointment scheduling/reminders, patient intake, insurance verification, prescription refills
- **CRITICAL NOTE:** ⚠️ **Smith.ai is NOT HIPAA-compliant** - cannot handle PHI for regulated healthcare. Best for veterinary, wellness, non-regulated practices.

#### Spas, Salons & Wellness Brands
- **Sub-types:** Hair salons, nail salons, spas, massage therapy, tattoo/piercing
- **Key Pain Points:** Complex scheduling, seasonal booking spikes, no-shows
- **Core Features:** 24/7 booking, appointment reminders, payment collection

### 4. Technology

#### IT Services & MSPs
- **Sub-types:** Managed service providers, IT consultancies, computer repair, network admin, cybersecurity, cloud services
- **Key Pain Points:** Technical teams lack customer-facing skills, need 24/7 coverage
- **Core Features:** After-hours helpdesk, support ticket collection, emergency routing

#### Software & SaaS Companies
- **Sub-types:** SaaS platforms, software development, app developers, enterprise/B2B/B2C software
- **Key Pain Points:** Industry growth ($908B by 2030), demo scheduling bottlenecks, support scaling
- **Core Features:** Lead qualification, demo scheduling, customer support, partner portal for MSPs/VARs

- **Core Features:** Tenant screening, maintenance management, rent collection, AI analytics (occupancy predictions)

### Content Strategy for Each Industry Page

**Every industry page should include:**

1. **Hero Section**
   - Industry-specific headline with pain point
   - Stat: "67% of [industry] clients judge you on responsiveness"
   - Primary CTA: "See How [Industry] Businesses Save $40K+ Annually"

2. **Pain Points Section** (3-5 bullet points)
   - Use industry-specific research from this document
   - Include statistics where available

3. **Key Features Section**
   - 6-8 features tailored to that industry
   - Example: For law firms - "Conflict checking before consultations"

4. **ROI Calculator** (Interactive)
   - "Calculate how much you could save vs. hiring in-house"
   - Input: Current staff costs, missed calls per week
   - Output: Annual savings, leads captured

5. **Social Proof**
   - Case studies from similar businesses
   - Testimonials from industry peers
   - Trust badges (industry associations)

6. **FAQ Section** (SEO-optimized)
   - "Do you integrate with [industry-specific CRM]?"
   - "Is this HIPAA-compliant?" (for medical)
   - "How fast can we get started?"

7. **CTA Variations**
   - Primary: "Start Free Trial"
   - Secondary: "See Pricing for [Industry]"
   - Tertiary: "Talk to a [Industry] Specialist"
