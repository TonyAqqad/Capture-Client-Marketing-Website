## Formatting Standards Applied

### Typography & Headings

- **Main Section Headings (H2)**: `class="text-2xl md:text-3xl font-bold text-white mt-12 mb-6"`
  - Large, bold, white text
  - Responsive sizing (2xl on mobile, 3xl on desktop)
  - Generous spacing (12 units top, 6 units bottom)

- **Subsection Headings (H3)**: `class="text-xl md:text-2xl font-semibold text-white mt-8 mb-4"`
  - Medium-large, semibold, white text
  - Responsive sizing (xl on mobile, 2xl on desktop)
  - Moderate spacing (8 units top, 4 units bottom)

### Body Content

- **Paragraphs**: `class="text-gray-300 leading-relaxed mb-6"`
  - Light gray text for readability
  - Relaxed line height for better reading
  - Consistent bottom margin

- **Strong Text**: `class="text-white font-semibold"`
  - White color to stand out
  - Semibold weight for emphasis

- **Emphasized Text**: `class="text-accent"`
  - Uses accent color for highlights
  - Draws attention to key points

### Lists

- **Unordered Lists**: `class="list-disc list-inside space-y-3 mb-6 text-gray-300"`
  - Disc bullet points
  - Bullets inside the list
  - Vertical spacing between items
  - Light gray text

- **Ordered Lists**: `class="list-decimal list-inside space-y-3 mb-6 text-gray-300"`
  - Decimal numbering
  - Numbers inside the list
  - Consistent spacing

- **List Items**: `class="leading-relaxed"`
  - Relaxed line height for readability

### Special Elements

- **Blockquotes**: `class="border-l-4 border-accent pl-6 py-2 my-8 bg-white/5 rounded-r-lg"`
  - Left border in accent color (4px)
  - Padding and subtle background
  - Rounded right corner
  - Vertical margin for separation

- **Links**: `class="text-accent hover:text-accent/80 underline"`
  - Accent color with underline
  - Hover effect (80% opacity)
  - Clear visual indicator

- **Horizontal Rules**: `class="border-white/10 my-12"`
  - Subtle white border (10% opacity)
  - Large vertical margin for section breaks

## Visual Hierarchy

The formatting creates a clear visual hierarchy:

1. **H2 headings** are the most prominent (white, bold, large)
2. **H3 headings** are secondary (white, semibold, medium)
3. **Body text** is readable (gray, relaxed leading)
4. **Emphasis** stands out (white for strong, accent for em)
5. **Lists** are well-spaced and scannable
6. **Links** are clearly identifiable
