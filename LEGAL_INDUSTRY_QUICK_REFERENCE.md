## COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

### HIPAA (CRITICAL for Personal Injury)
- Applies when handling Protected Health Information (PHI)
- Business Associate Agreement (BAA) required
- Encrypted communications mandatory
- Staff training on HIPAA regulations
- Report breaches within 60 days
- Fines: $100M+ collected since 2003

### Attorney-Client Privilege
- All communications confidential
- Secure storage required
- Encrypted email/messaging
- Vendor NDAs

### Conflict Checking
- Must check before taking clients
- ABA compliance required
- Search across all contacts/matters
- Automatic during intake

### Data Security
- End-to-end encryption
- SOC-2 compliance
- Regular security audits
- Multi-factor authentication

## PRACTICE AREA INTAKE QUESTIONS

### Personal Injury
- When did the accident occur?
- What injuries did you sustain?
- Have you received medical treatment?
- Do you have insurance?
- Have you spoken to the other party's insurance?
- **HIPAA compliance required**

### Criminal Defense
- Have you been arrested or charged?
- What are the charges?
- Court dates?
- Currently in custody?
- **24/7 emergency response critical**

### Family Law
- Married or separated?
- Children involved?
- What are you seeking?
- Safety concerns?

### Immigration Law
- Current immigration status?
- Visa type/expiration?
- Family members who are US citizens?
- Removal proceedings?
- **Multi-language support critical**
