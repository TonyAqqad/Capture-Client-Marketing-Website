**Recommendation:** Make "Who We Serve" the FIRST or SECOND nav item (high priority).

### 2. **Integrations Deserve Top-Level Visibility**
Smith.ai puts "Integrations" under "Solutions" but features it prominently. With 7,000+ integrations, this is a massive SEO opportunity.

**Recommendation:** Give Integrations its own top-level nav item with mega-menu showing:
- Featured native integrations (GoHighLevel, Calendly, etc.)
- Categories (CRM, Scheduling, Field Service, etc.)
- Industry-specific integrations (Legal tools, Medical EMR, etc.)

### 3. **Partner Programs Build Distribution**
Smith.ai has 300+ active partners. Their 4-tier program covers:
- Affiliates (easy entry)
- Resellers (agencies)
- Wholesale (large operators)
- Technology (integration partners)

**Recommendation:** Launch with 3 tiers (Affiliate, Agency, Technology) to start building partner network.

### 4. **Pricing Transparency Builds Trust**
Smith.ai shows:
- Clear per-call pricing for AI product
- Clear monthly tiers for human product
- All add-on pricing listed
- No hidden fees messaging
- Guarantees prominently displayed

**Recommendation:** Match or exceed this transparency. Consider adding ROI calculator.

### 5. **Multi-Column Mega-Menus Increase Engagement**
Smith.ai uses multi-column dropdowns to show more options without overwhelming users.

**Recommendation:** Implement 2-3 column mega-menus for "Who We Serve" and "Integrations".
