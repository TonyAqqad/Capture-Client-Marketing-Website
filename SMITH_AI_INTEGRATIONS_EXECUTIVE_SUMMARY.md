## Top 5 Most Important Integrations

### 1. **Zapier** (Automation Platform)
- **Category:** Automation
- **Impact:** Connects to 5,000+ additional apps
- **Key Features:**
  - Automate data transfer to CRMs, spreadsheets, email lists
  - Initiate outbound calls via Zap actions
  - Launch Outreach Campaign calls from other apps
  - Eliminates manual data entry
- **Works With:** Virtual Receptionists, AI Receptionist, Web Chat, Outreach Campaigns

### 2. **HubSpot** (CRM & Marketing)
- **Category:** CRM
- **Impact:** Top CRM for SMBs and agencies
- **Key Features:**
  - Automatic contact creation from calls and chats
  - Call notes and chat transcript logging
  - Contact information capture
  - Outreach campaign integration
- **Works With:** Virtual Receptionists, AI Receptionist, Web Chat, Outreach Campaigns

### 3. **Salesforce** (Enterprise CRM)
- **Category:** CRM
- **Impact:** #1 enterprise CRM platform
- **Key Features:**
  - Automatic contact information updates
  - Post-call summary notes sync
  - Native integration (no middleware)
- **Works With:** Virtual Receptionists, AI Receptionist, Web Chat, Outreach Campaigns

### 4. **Calendly** (Scheduling)
- **Category:** Scheduling
- **Impact:** Most popular scheduling tool for SMBs
- **Key Features:**
  - Real-time appointment booking
  - Lead qualification before scheduling
  - Pre-call intake forms and payment collection
  - Calendly Routing support
  - Automatic appointment reminders
- **Works With:** Virtual Receptionists, AI Receptionist, Web Chat, Outreach Campaigns

### 5. **Slack** (Team Communication)
- **Category:** Automation
- **Impact:** Critical for internal team workflows
- **Key Features:**
  - Real-time call transfer notifications
  - Automatic call summaries to channels
  - Chat transcripts routed to Slack
  - Callback request notifications
  - 24/7 availability
- **Works With:** Virtual Receptionists, AI Receptionist, Web Chat, Outreach Campaigns
