## 4. TOP 5 CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION TACTICS

### Tactic #1: Tiered Pricing with Anchoring (Smith.ai Model)

**Smith.ai's Pricing Psychology**:

```
AI RECEPTIONIST PLANS:

[Starter]          [Basic] ‚òÖ         [Pro]            [Enterprise]
$95/mo             $270/mo            $800/mo          Custom
50 calls           150 calls          500 calls        Unlimited
$2.40 overage      $2.30 overage      $2.10 overage    Volume pricing

MOST POPULAR ‚Üí     [Basic Plan highlighted]
```

**Why This Works**:
1. **Anchoring**: Enterprise price makes Pro look affordable
2. **Decoy Effect**: Starter has worst unit economics ($1.90/call) ‚Üí pushes to Basic
3. **Progressive Overage Reduction**: Incentivizes upgrading instead of paying overages
4. **"Most Popular" Badge**: Social proof + default choice reduction
