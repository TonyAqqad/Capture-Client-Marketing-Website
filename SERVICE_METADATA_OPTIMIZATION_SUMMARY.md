## Best Practices Applied

### 1. Pain-Point Driven
Every meta description starts with a customer pain point:
- "Missing calls and losing leads?"
- "Wasting money on Google Ads?"
- "Stop chasing leads"

### 2. Phone CTAs
Every description includes phone number:
- "Free demo: (865) 346-3339"
- "Free strategy call: (865) 346-3339"

### 3. Trust Signals
Every description includes credibility markers:
- "Trusted by 500+ businesses"
- "Google Partner certified"
- "4:1 average ROAS"

### 4. Specific Benefits
Titles include measurable outcomes:
- "24/7 Lead Capture"
- "ROI-Focused PPC"
- "Lower Cost Leads"

### 5. Target Audience
Every title specifies who it's for:
- "for Small Business"
- "for Service Businesses"
- "for Contractors"
