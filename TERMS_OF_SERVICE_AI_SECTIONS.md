## SECTION 2: TCPA Compliance and Consent

```markdown
## Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) Compliance

By providing your phone number and using our services, you provide your
prior express written consent to receive:

- Informational and service-related calls, texts, and voice messages
- Marketing and promotional communications
- Automated calls made using an automatic telephone dialing system (ATDS),
  artificial intelligence, or prerecorded/artificial voices

### Your Rights

**Consent is Optional:**
Consent is not required as a condition of purchase or use of our services.

**How to Opt Out:**
You may revoke consent at any time by:
- Replying "STOP" to any text message
- Calling (865) 346-3339 and requesting to be added to our Do Not Call list
- Emailing team@captureclient.net with "OPT OUT" in the subject line

**Processing Time:**
We will honor opt-out requests within 10 business days as required by FCC
rules effective April 11, 2025.

**Do Not Call Registry:**
We comply with the National Do Not Call Registry. If your number is
registered, you should not receive marketing calls from us unless you have
provided express consent or we have an established business relationship
with you.

**Calling Hours:**
We only make calls between 8:00 AM and 9:00 PM in your local time zone,
as required by the TCPA.

**Your Contact Information:**
You are responsible for providing accurate contact information. If you
provide someone else's phone number, you represent that you have
authority to provide consent on their behalf.

### Our Compliance Commitment

We take TCPA compliance seriously and maintain strict policies to protect
your rights:
- We scrub our calling lists against the National Do Not Call Registry
  every 31 days
- We maintain an internal Do Not Call list and honor all opt-out requests
- We train our AI systems to recognize and honor opt-out requests
- We maintain detailed records of consent and communications

### Penalties

Violations of the TCPA can result in penalties of $500-$1,500 per call.
We are committed to compliance and have implemented systems and procedures
to ensure we respect your communication preferences.
```

```markdown
You have the right to opt out of communications from us at any time.

### National Do Not Call Registry

We honor the National Do Not Call Registry maintained by the Federal Trade
Commission.

If your number is registered on the National Do Not Call Registry, you
should not receive marketing calls from us unless:
- You have provided express consent to receive calls, OR
- We have an established business relationship with you

To register your number on the National Do Not Call Registry:
- Visit: www.donotcall.gov
- Call: 1-888-382-1222

### Partial Opt-Out

You may opt out of:
- Marketing calls only (continue receiving service/informational calls)
- Text messages only (continue receiving phone calls)
- AI calls only (receive human calls instead)

Specify your preference when opting out.

### Verification

After opting out, you should not receive additional calls or messages from
us within 10 business days. If you continue to receive unwanted
communications after 10 business days, please contact us immediately at
team@captureclient.net.
```

## SECTION 6: State-Specific Compliance

```markdown
## State-Specific Compliance

We comply with state-specific laws and regulations regarding AI technology,
call recording, and consumer protection.

### California Residents

**AI Disclosure:**
Our AI voice agents will identify themselves as automated at the beginning
of calls: "I am an automated virtual assistant from Capture Client."

**Call Recording:**
California requires all-party consent for call recording. You will be
explicitly notified and asked for consent before any recording begins.

**Privacy Rights:**
California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer
Privacy Act (CCPA). See our Privacy Policy for details.

### Colorado Residents

**AI Act Compliance (Effective February 1, 2026):**
High-risk AI systems are disclosed on our website. You will be notified
before AI makes any consequential decisions affecting you.

**Your Rights:**
Right to know when interacting with AI, right to human review of AI
decisions, and right to opt out of AI interactions.

### Illinois Residents

**Biometric Information Privacy Act (BIPA):**
If we collect voiceprint biometric data from Illinois residents, we will
obtain separate written consent before collection.

**Your Rights:**
Right to informed consent, right to know purpose and retention period,
right to deletion of voiceprint data, and right to sue for BIPA violations.

**Call Recording:**
Illinois requires all-party consent for call recording.

### Florida Residents

**Calling Hours:**
Florida law restricts calls to 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM (stricter than federal
8 AM - 9 PM requirement).

### Other Two-Party Consent States

If you are located in Connecticut, Delaware, Maryland, Massachusetts,
Michigan, Montana, Nevada, New Hampshire, Pennsylvania, Vermont, or
Washington, all parties must consent to call recording. You will be
notified at the beginning of recorded calls.

### Multi-State Compliance

When calls involve parties in different states, we apply the most
restrictive state's requirements to ensure full compliance.
```
