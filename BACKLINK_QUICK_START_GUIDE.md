## COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

❌ Buying links from fiverr/cheap services (Google penalty risk)
❌ Only pursuing high DA, ignoring relevance (Voice AI niche > generic DA 80 site)
❌ Building too many links too fast (looks unnatural)
❌ Forgetting local focus (Tennessee links = local SEO gold)
❌ Not tracking results (use Ahrefs/SEMrush weekly)

✅ Focus on editorial links from real sites
✅ Prioritize niche relevance
✅ Build steadily (5-10 links/month)
✅ Mix local + national opportunities
✅ Track everything
